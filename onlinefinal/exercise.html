MOVE
To have the ability to move your body freely is a blessing we often forget. And this is what makes our bodies function properly. Nothing but our excuses stop us. And that is awful. Here are some tips and tricks you might need to get started.

JUST START
The beginnings are always the hardest. But nothing worth it comes easy. It is only your mind that plays tricks on you and makes you feel you cannot achieve a fitness goal.

It is scientifically proved that exercising has many positive aspects on people such as:
-Sharper memory and thinking
-Higher self-esteem
-Better sleep
-More energy
-Increases your level of endorphins
-Limits the effect of stress on your brain

WORKOUT BUDDY
You can also share your fitness journey with a friend who can become your workout buddy. This will make it more enjoyable and keep you going forward. In the best case this buddy will challenge you beyond your limits.
