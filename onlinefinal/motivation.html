Are you looking for me?
It is hard to find motivation to exercise now and then ... but that is something normal. Discipline is the power that keeps us going in the direction towards our goals. You cannot always be motivated. But our future is based on what we do daily. Our everyday choices determine our results.
Regrets
It is better to have a 15-minute-workout than no workout at all. As it is a well-known fact: “You only regret the workouts you didn’t do”. So why are you still reading this and not doing anything to become a more active person.

Think about it…
It is a very common habit of students to watch Netflix in their free time and there is nothing bad about it. But imagine if you spend half of that time for a quick workout. This in long term will pay off, trust me.

YOU
You know - it is your life based on your decisions. You are the only reason why you don’t have that dream body. You are the best thing you will ever work on. Imagine what can be achieved if you start now – right where you are.
